Expertise

I'm a creative and versatile UX designer with experience across a number of UX disciplines. My strengths include user research, analysis, information architecture, wireframes and prototyping, usability testing, and strategy.

User research

Sampling, interviewing and moderating to understand behaviours and motivations

Analysis

Heuristic, metrics and task analysis to benchmark and surface current behaviours

Information architecture

Auditing, organising, categorising, labelling and mapping to define digital structures

Wireframes/prototyping

Sketching, drawing and creating low and high fidelity designs to define how users interact with an interface

Usability testing

Remote, face-to-face, moderated and un-moderated iterative testing to refine products and processes

Strategy

Developing frameworks to improve, measure and manage the experience across channels and devices

My story

I'm Head of User Experience at The University of Nottingham, where I work to improve the way student information is handled and represented online across campuses in the UK, China, and Malaysia. Prior to that I was a UX Designer at G2G3 Digital (part of Capita Plc) in Sheffield, and web content manager at creative consultancy The Workshop Ltd. I've worked with a diverse client base including O2 Telefónica, The Nuclear Decommissioning Authority, Houses of Parliament, and Sheffield Hallam University.

A Sheffield native, I returned to study design communications in the late 1990's, graduating with distinction. I quickly built a career in digital, with roles ranging from web content developer to digital services manager. I've worked for some of the largest universities in the UK, to award winning creative digital consultancies. I completed and gained a distinction for a Masters in Design Management, where my focus was UX theory, methodology, principles and practice.
