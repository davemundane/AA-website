Axisweb21 Campaign

Axisweb21 was designed as a campaign piece to promote 21 years of contemporary arts organisation Axisweb. I led on the development of an interactive feature, designed to be easily shareable across social media and to keep visitors engaged on the site.

Social engagement

I sourced and commissioned digital agency Enjoy Digital to produce the feature, working closely with their product managers, designers, and developers, and internal teams at Axisweb to ensure the project ran on time and met the brief. The feature was supported by a social media campaign, which pointed users towards the page and culminated with a Twitter party at Axisweb HQ.
