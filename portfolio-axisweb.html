Axisweb website refresh

Contemporary arts organisation Axisweb wanted to redevelop their company website, to align with a new corporate brand that had recently been introduced.

Agile development

I worked closely with commissioned agency Code Computerlove, to develop the new site from scratch. I sat with the project team at their Manchester offices over a six week period, acting as 'product owner' during the agile development process.



I signed off user journeys, hard-coded content, core functionality and visuals for the new website, to deliver the best product possible within time frame and budget.
